## Decisions

| Decision | Source ref |
|---|---|

| `changeOrigin: false` — backend receives original Host header; no virtual-host routing. | TECHNICAL_GUIDE §11.4; vite.config.ts block comment |

Strategy A removes the preflight by collapsing to same-origin. | TECHNICAL_GUIDE §15 ADR-002; `orchestrator-state/memory/official-doc-notes/P01-S03-T002-cors-2026-05-13.md` RESOLVED line |
