### Routing algorithm observed

- Message tokenization: re.findall(r"\w+", msg.lower()) → set intersection with subagent_topics
- Score = cardinality of intersection
- Tie-break: larger len(subagent_topics) wins, then name ascending
- Fallback: RoutingDecision(selected_subagent=None, score=0, matched_topics=[], fallback_used=True)
