=== RAG Feature Verbose Logging Contract (ENABLE_VERBOSE_LOGGING=true) ===

Logger implementation (frontend/src/features/rag/data/logger.ts):
  - logVerbose(): gated by import.meta.env.VITE_ENABLE_VERBOSE_LOGGING === "true" → console.info
  - logWarn(): always fires → console.warn
  - logError(): always fires → console.error

Operations instrumented (in ragRepository.ts):
  BEFORE: logVerbose("rag.repo.listDocuments.start", {collection_id, status, has_cursor, limit})
  AFTER:  logVerbose("rag.repo.listDocuments.ok", {count, has_next_cursor, request_id})
  ERROR:  logWarn/logError("rag.repo.listDocuments.forbidden|rate_limited|server_error|error", {...})

  BEFORE: logVerbose("rag.repo.uploadDocument.start", {mime_type, size_bucketed, language, collection_id})
  AFTER:  logVerbose("rag.repo.uploadDocument.ok", {kind, doc_id, status, language, request_id})
  ERROR:  logWarn/logError("rag.repo.uploadDocument.validation_error|too_large|error...", {...})

  BEFORE: logVerbose("rag.repo.indexDocument.start", {doc_id}) [in _ragRepositoryExtras.ts]
  AFTER:  logVerbose("rag.repo.indexDocument.ok", {kind, job_id, status, request_id})
  ERROR:  logWarn/logError("rag.repo.indexDocument.in_progress|not_found|forbidden|error", {...})

Note: In vitest environment, VITE_ENABLE_VERBOSE_LOGGING is not injected from shell 
ENABLE_VERBOSE_LOGGING. Verbose gating works correctly in browser dev server.
Error/warn paths (logWarn, logError) are always visible and appear in vitest output.
